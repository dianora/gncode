Sessions and Candidates

Seizing the technological opportunity

Our objective is to prepare the future generation of Guinean computer scientists and professionals ready to take up the challenges and seize the opportunities inherent in the highly technological future for which Guinea is inevitably destined.

Learning to code

Our training programs include curricula such as CSBridge, an excellent introductory computer programming curriculum based on pedagogical techniques that have been used at Stanford University for over 20 years.

Towards a more productive and technological Guinea

In the following years, we want to: Intensify computer education in Guinea, build and operate learning centers in all the prefectures of Guinea, and train teachers, students and professionals ready for the job market.
